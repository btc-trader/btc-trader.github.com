About

BTC Trader — is bitcoin-focused opensource charting, technical analysis and trading application built on top of the Chartsy and NetBeans platforms.

Latest version 1.4.1.1 includes:

  • Live data feed from bitcoincharts.com
  • Daily data feed from Yahoo Finance
  • Rich collection of technical-analysis tools
  • Facebook and Twitter integration

BTC Trader is absolutely open and free of charge and licensed under the terms of the GNU General Public Licence of version 2.
