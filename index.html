About

BTC Trader — is bitcoin-focused opensource charting, technical analysis and trading application built on top of the Chartsy and NetBeans platforms.

Latest version 1.4.2 includes:

  • Live data feed from bitcoincharts.com
  • Daily data feed from Yahoo Finance
  • Rich collection of technical-analysis tools
    • 5 chart types
    • 8 annotation tools
    • >70 indicators
  • Facebook and Twitter integration
  • Automatic software updater

BTC Trader is absolutely open and free of charge and licensed under the terms of GNU General Public Licence version 2.

Feel free to discuss BTC Trader and get support at the community forum.

Also you can follow my twitter @bitcoinfreak and join my network on Google+ Mr. Coinfreak.
